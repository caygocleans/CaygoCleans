**Target length:** 50–55 seconds

---

**[0:00 – 0:03] HOOK**
"This is why your home never feels truly clean — even after you've just cleaned it."

---

[B-roll: Close-up of dust visibly falling from a shelf down to a floor.]

**[0:03 – 0:35] THE TIP**
"Most beginners clean the floor first. Vacuum, mop, done. Then they wipe the shelves and counters — and all that dust falls straight back down onto the clean floor.

The fix is simple: always clean top to bottom.

Start with the highest surfaces in the room. Shelves, countertops, tables. Let the dust fall. Then — and only then — you clean the floor.

Do this one thing differently and your home will stay cleaner, longer, with less effort."

## SHORT #2 — "Clean Your Whole Bathroom in Under 10 Minutes"
**Target length:** 55–60 seconds

---

[B-roll: Timer starting on a phone. Quick cuts of each cleaning step as they're described.]

**[0:00 – 0:03] HOOK**
"I'm going to show you how to clean your entire bathroom in under 10 minutes."

---

[B-roll: Each step shown in quick, satisfying cuts — mirror wipe, sink scrub, toilet clean, shower spray, floor mop. Keep cuts fast and rhythmic.]

**[0:03 – 0:48] THE METHOD**
"Here's the exact order.

Step one — mirror. Spray your cloth, not the mirror. Wipe in an S-shape top to bottom. No streaks.

Step two — sink and tap. Spray, scrub, rinse. Focus on the tap handles — that's where bacteria builds up.

Step three — toilet. Squirt cleaner inside the bowl and let it sit. Wipe the outside first — tank, lid, seat, base. Then scrub the bowl and flush.

Step four — shower. Spray the walls, wait 30 seconds, wipe down.

Step five — floor. Sweep first, then a quick mop.

That's it. Start the timer."
