# Instagram Carousel — Week 1
## "5 Cleaning Mistakes You're Probably Making Right Now"
**Format:** 7 slides | **Platform:** Instagram

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS**
> - Build each slide in Canva using CaygoCleans brand colours: Navy (#1B3E6F), Blue (#2B7EC1), Teal (#48C4D8), White (#FFFFFF)
> - Font pairing: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter or Montserrat (body)
> - Keep text minimal — the slide should be readable in 2 seconds
> - Swipe-bait: end slide 1 with something that makes them want to tap through
> - Each slide should feel complete on its own in case someone screenshots it

## SLIDE 2 — Mistake #1

**Mistake label (small, teal):**
MISTAKE #1

**Headline:**
Cleaning the floor first.

**Body text:**
You vacuum, then wipe the shelves —
and the dust falls right back down.

**Fix (bold):**
Always clean top to bottom.
Shelves first. Floor last.

**Design notes:**
- White background
- Navy headline, dark grey body text
- Teal used for the "MISTAKE #1" label and "Fix" line
- Small icon: broom or downward arrow

---

## SLIDE 3 — Mistake #2

**Mistake label (small, teal):**
MISTAKE #2

**Headline:**
Spraying cleaner directly
on surfaces.

**Body text:**
Spraying dusty surfaces creates
a muddy paste that's harder to remove.

**Fix (bold):**
Spray your cloth, not the surface.
Dust dry first, then wipe wet.

**Design notes:**
- Light blue/sky background (#EBF5FB)
- Navy headline
- Small icon: spray bottle with an X, then a cloth with a tick

---

## SLIDE 4 — Mistake #3

**Mistake label (small, teal):**
MISTAKE #3

**Headline:**
Wiping the toilet seat
before the outside.

**Body text:**
Most people scrub the bowl last —
but clean the seat first.
That spreads bacteria the wrong way.

**Fix (bold):**
Outside first. Bowl last. Always.
Tank → lid → seat → base → bowl → flush.

**Design notes:**
- White background
- This is the "No. 3" teased on Slide 1 — make it feel worth the swipe
- Small icon: toilet or checkmark sequence

---

## SLIDE 5 — Mistake #4

**Mistake label (small, teal):**
MISTAKE #4

**Headline:**
Using the same cloth
for every surface.

**Body text:**
One cloth for the toilet,
the sink, and the kitchen counter
is spreading germs — not removing them.

**Fix (bold):**
Colour-code your cloths.
One colour per room. No crossover.

**Design notes:**
- Navy background (#1B3E6F)
- White text
- Teal accent for the fix
- Small icon: three different coloured squares/cloths

---

## SLIDE 6 — Mistake #5

**Mistake label (small, teal):**
MISTAKE #5

**Headline:**
Cleaning without a system.

**Body text:**
Moving randomly from room to room
means you'll miss things,
repeat work, and run out of energy.

**Fix (bold):**
Pick a room. Finish it completely.
Then move to the next one.

**Design notes:**
- Light blue/sky background (#EBF5FB)
- Navy headline
- Small icon: checklist or room layout
